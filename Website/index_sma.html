About CommentGuard
CommentGuard is a user-friendly web application designed to detect offensive language and protect online communities. From social media platforms to websites, CommentGuard ensures a safer digital environment for all users.
How it works
Content Monitoring
CommentGuard scans text content in real-time for potentially harmful language.
Automatic Filtering
Offensive comments are automatically filtered out before they can reach the audience.
Custom Settings
Users can customize the severity level of filters based on their platform's requirements.
Features
1
Real-Time Detection
Identifies and removes harmful content as it is posted.
2
Multilingual Support
Capable of analyzing content in multiple languages to ensure broad coverage.
3
Customizable Filters
Provides flexibility to adjust filters according to specific community standards.
4
Scalable Solution
Adaptable for use on various digital platforms, from small forums to large social networks.
